User manual for students

Schedule

What steps are necessary to create an event in the timetable?
  1. Click the «CATS» logo at the left corner of the screen to move to the schedule page.
  2. Click on any cell for the certain date.
  3. Fill in the required data.
  4. Click the [Save] button to save changes.
How to edit an event?
  1. Find the desired event and click the «…» button.
  2. The following options such as Edit and Delete appear.
  3. Click the [Edit] option.
  4. In the appeared window edit the necessary data.
  5. Click the [Save] button to save your changes.
How to delete an event?
  1. Find the required event and click the «…» button.
  2. The following options such as Edit and Delete appear.
  3. Click the [Delete] option.
  4. In the appeared window, click the [Yes] button to delete or click the [No] button to cancel.

Courses

How to navigate to the right course?
  1. Click on the [Courses] button in the top bar.
  2. Click on [Select an item] in the opened panel.
  3. Select the required course in the list of course.
  4. It’s possible to navigate to subsections of the Course in the opened page.

News

How to check the news of the particular course?
  1. Navigate to the required course.
  2. Move to the News subsection.
  3. The news is on the opened page.

Lectures

How to download the files attached to a lecture?
  1. Navigate to the required course.
  2. Navigate to the Lectures subsection.
  3. Find the required lecture and press the [Files] icon.
  4. In the opened window, mark the necessary files and press the [Download] button.
  5. The marked files are downloaded to your device.
How to find out the information about lectures attendance?
  1. Navigate to the right course.
  2. Move to the Лекции subsection.
  3. Click on the Lectures attendance tab.
  4. When the page opens, the table displaying group's students and their lecture attendance is present.

Educational complex

How to view an existing Educational complex?
  1. Navigate to the required course.
  2. Move to the Educational complex subsection.
  3. Find the required educational complex and click on it.
  4. The educational complex with sections and subsections is available in the new window. Click on the name of the required section or subsection to view it.
  5. Click the [Cancel] button to close the educational complex.
How to navigate to the adaptive learning section?
  1. Navigate to the required course.
  2. Move to the Educational complex subsection.
  3. Find the required educational complex and click on it.
  4. The educational complex opens in a new window. Click the [Adaptive Learning] button.
  5. Select the desired learning algorithm: The advanced Learning Algorithm or The basic Learning Algorithm in the appeared window.

If You have choosen the Basic Learning Algorithm:

  1. (If you have not taken adaptive learning before) Press [Take the pre-test] button in the opened window.
  2. (If you have not taken adaptive learning before) Pass the test select an answer, click the [Answer] button to select an answer, or click the [Skip] button to leave a question for the end of a test.
  3. (If you have not taken adaptive learning before) After answering all the questions, click the [Complete Test] button to proceed to the learning.
  4. The next section of the educational complex opens. Study it and take a quiz on the material you have learnt.

 

If You have chosen the Advanced Learning Algorithm:

  1. Familiarize with documents. Click the [Next Document] button to move to the next document (or click the [Previous Document] button to move to the previous one).
  2. After you have become familiar with every document in the section and the pre-set amount of time has gone by, the [To Tests] button is enabled so that you can move to the tests.
  3. After the test opens, select the necessary answer to a question and press the [Answer] button to select the answer or the [Skip] button to leave this question for the end of the test.
  4. When you have answered all the questions, click the [End Test] button.

  1. Navigate to the required course.
  2. Move to the Files subsection.
  3. Click on the [Download an archive] icon to download all files in an archive.

  1. Navigate to the required course.
  2. Move to the Files subsection.
  3. Click on the desired file, select the download location of the file in your device.

Tests

How to take a test?
  1. Navigate to the required course.
  2. Move to the Tests subsection.
  3. Find the required test (if the required test is not on the list, it means that a teacher hasn’t opened access to it) and click the [Take a test] button.
  4. Press the [Next] button in the opened page.
  5. In the opened test, select an answer option and click the [Answer] button (to save the selected answer) or the [Skip] button (to skip the question and answer it later).
How to view my test results?
  1. Navigate to the required course.
  2. Move to the Tests subsection.
  3. Click the Test Results tab.
  4. Select the required types of tests on the appeared page.
  5. The page displays the test results for the selected tests.

Course projects

How to view and select the topic for my term project?

You can select a topic for your course project if You haven’t chosen any topic or Your teacher hasn’t assigned one to You.

  1. Navigate to the required course.
  2. Move to the Course projects subsection.
  3. The page displays all the topics for course.
  4. Find the required topic and click on the [Select the topic] icon.
  5. Click the [Select] button on the opened page.
  6. The given topic assigns to You, to cancel the assignment contact the teacher of this course.
How to find the right course, student, or group in the topics list?
  1. Navigate to the required course.
  2. Move to the Course projects subsection.
  3. Click on the [Search for a topic, students, a group] field.
  4. Enter the information You want to search for.
  5. The system searches based on the information entered. To reset filtering, erase the entered data from the field.
How to download a task sheet?
  1. Navigate to the required course.
  2. Move to the Course projects subsection.
  3. Click on the Task sheet tab.
  4. Select the required topic and click on the [Download the assignment sheet] icon.
How to view the percentages, percentage results, consultation visit statistics?
  1. Navigate to the desired course.
  2. Move to the Course projects subsection.
  3. Click on the Percentages, Percentage Results or Consultation visit tab.
  4. The opened page displays the required information.
How to send a course project for submitting?

How to edit a submitted course project for a review?
  1. Navigate to the desired course.
  2. Move to the Course projects subsection.
  3. Click on the Work submission tab.
  4. Find the required work and click on the [Edit] icon.
  5. In the opened window, edit the required information and press the [Submit Paper] button.
How to delete a submitted course project for a review?
  1. Navigate to the desired course.
  2. Move to the Course projects subsection.
  3. Click on the Work submission tab.
  4. Find the required work and click on the [Delete project] icon.

Graduation project

Why is the Graduation project section unavailable to me?

The Graduation project section is only available to students whose graduation year is the same as the current year.

How to view and select a graduation project theme?

You can select a graduation project theme if You hasn’t selected any theme or Your teacher hasn’t assigned one to You.

  1. Click on the Graduation project section in the top bar.
  2. The page displays all the themes for graduation projects.
  3. Find the theme you want and click on the [Select] icon.
  4. In the opened window, click the [Select] button.
  5. The given theme assigns to You, to cancel the assignment contact the teacher of this course.
How to find the necessary theme, student, or group in the theme list?
  1. Click on the Graduation project section in the top bar.
  2. Click on the [Search for a theme, students, group] field.
  3. Enter the information you want to search for.
  4. The system searches based on the information entered. To reset the filtering, erase the entered data from the field.
How to download the task sheet?
  1. Click on the Graduation project section in the top bar.
  2. Move to the Task sheet tab.
  3. Select the desired topic and click on the [Download the task sheet] icon.
How to view the percentages, percentage results, consultation visit statistics?
  1. Click on the Graduation project section in the top bar.
  2. Click on the Percentages, Percentage Results or Consultation visit tab.
  3. The opened page displays the information you need.
